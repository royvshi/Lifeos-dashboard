📲 How to Auto-Update Every Week with iPhone Shortcuts
1
Copy your wallpaper URL
2
Create Automation
Open Shortcuts app → Automation tab → New Automation → Time of Day → 6:00 AM → Repeat "Daily" → Select "Run Immediately" → "Create New Shortcut"
3
Add these actions
3.1 "Get Contents of URL" → paste the URL from step 1
3.2 "Set Wallpaper Photo" → choose "Lock Screen"
⚠ Important:
In "Set Wallpaper Photo", tap the arrow (→) to show options → disable both "Crop to Subject" and "Show Preview"
This prevents iOS from cropping and asking for confirmation each time
💡 Pro tip: On iOS 16+, go to Settings → Wallpaper → Customise to also add a Lock Screen Widget showing your weeks remaining using Widgetsmith or Data Jar — no wallpaper swap needed.
